Great Games to play

There are many different games in the world, each made to satisfy a certain kind of audience. I like PVP, First-Person-shooter, and casual games. Here are a few games I reccommend you play.


Game 1: Free Fire

Price: Free



Garena Free Fire, also known as Free Fire, is a battle royale game, developed by 111dots Studio and published by Garena for Android and iOS. It became the most downloaded mobile game globally in 2019. In August 2021, Free Fire set a record with over 150 million daily active users globally.



Game 2: Minecraft


Price: $26.50

Minecraft is a singleplayer survival game. You spawn in the overworld and gather resources and make tools and weapons. The day is peaceful, but the night is filled with monsters. You can enchant weapons and expand your base. You then travel to different dimensions gathering recources, then slay the ender dragon to beat the game. You can make a server and invite friends to play with you as well!



Game 3: Valorant



Price: Free (But a Lot of Computer Space)



Valorant is a 5v5 First-Person-Shooter game. At the start of each round, you recharge your abilities and buy guns. You are either on the attacker team or defender team. If you are on the defender team, you need to find an object called a spike, plant it, and protect it before time runs out. If you are the attackers, you have to defuse the spike that has been planted. If one team elimintaes all the players on the other team, the team left standing wins. Each character is called an agent, and they have special abilities. For example, the agent I use is called Chill. He is archer. He has special arrows like explosive arrows and an arrow that can scan for oppnets. Each agent has their stregths and weaknesses. You need to work together and use the abilities to win the match.
